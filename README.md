# blinkit-dashboard
📌 Project Overview

The Blinkit Sales Dashboard is an interactive Power BI project that provides a comprehensive analysis of Blinkit's sales performance. It helps visualize key business metrics such as total sales, average sales, product performance, outlet analysis, customer ratings, and sales trends across different outlet types and locations.

The dashboard enables stakeholders to monitor business performance, identify top-performing product categories, and make data-driven decisions through interactive visualizations and filters.

🎯 Objectives
Analyze overall sales performance.
Track sales trends by outlet establishment year.
Compare sales across outlet sizes and locations.
Evaluate outlet performance using key business KPIs.
Identify top-selling product categories.
Understand customer preferences based on fat content and item types.

🔍 Key Insights
Total sales reached $1 Million.
Supermarket Type 1 generated the highest sales.
Tier 3 outlets contributed the largest share of revenue.
Medium-sized outlets performed better than small and high-sized outlets.
Regular Fat products generated higher sales than Low Fat products.
Fruits & Vegetables and Snack Foods were among the top-selling categories.
Average customer rating remained 3.9, indicating positive customer satisfaction.
